Gyudon

Ingredients

  • 0.5 onion
  • 1 green onion
  • 227 g thinly sliced beef
  • 120 ml dashi
  • 2 tbsp sake
  • 2 tbsp mirin
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 250 g cooked rice

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients

To prepare the ingredients

  1. Thinly slice 0.5 onions
  2. Cut 1 green onion into thin slices
  3. Cut the meat into 7 cm pieces
  4. In an unheated fryingpan, add 120 ml dashi, 2 tbsp sake, 2 tbsp mirin, 3 tbsp soy sauce, and 1 tbsp sugar. Mix to combine
  5. Add the onion slices and spread them through out the pan, seperating the onion layers
  6. Add meat on top of the onions

To cook

  1. Cover the pan with a lid and turn on the heat to medium and start cooking
  2. When the meat has browned, skim off the scum and fat with a fine-mesh skimmer. Turn down the heat to a simmer and continue cooking covered for 3-4 minutes
  3. Sprinkle green onions on top and cook for another minute

To serve

  1. Drizzle some of the sauce from the pan onto the rice
  2. Put the beef and onion mixture on top of the rice

Enjoy!
