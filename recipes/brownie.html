Brownie

Ingredients

  • 71 g unsalted butter
  • 249 g granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs + 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 64 g vegetable oil
  • 75 g cocoa powder
  • 63 g AP flour
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 128 g semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 160 Celsius. Line 8x8 metal pan with parchment paper and spray with nonstick cooking spray
  2. Add butter and sugar to a microwave-safe bowl. Microwave for about 1 minute. Whisk in the eggs, egg yolk, and vanilla. Stir in the oil and cocoa powder
  3. Stir in the flour, cornstarch, and salt until combined. Stir in the chocolate chips
  4. Spread the brownie batter into the prepared pan. Place into oven for 30 minutes

Enjoy!
